Quoralis

As one of ten finalists in the C-Prize competition, Quoralis is developing a new product that will help indicate fall risks in the elderly using a light and unobtrusive wearable device. Our goal is to improve the quality of elderly care and prevent falls in order to increase the number of healthy years we all live.

FallCast

FallCast is the product that Quoralis is developing, intended to be worn by independent elderly, as well as those in assisted living situations. It is a wearable insole device which tracks the wearer's motion patterns and uses this information to warn their friends, family and caregivers as soon as it detects they are more likely to have a fall in the next few weeks.
